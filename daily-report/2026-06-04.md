## Articles & Blog Posts

**[Microsoft Cancels Internal Claude Code Licenses, Pushes Copilot CLI](https://www.windowscentral.com/microsoft/microsoft-cancels-claude-code-licenses-shifting-developers-to-github-copilot-cli-a-move-likely-driven-by-financial-motives)** by *Windows Central* — Microsoft is discontinuing Claude Code for its Experiences & Devices division (Windows, Microsoft 365, Teams, Outlook, Surface) by June 30.
