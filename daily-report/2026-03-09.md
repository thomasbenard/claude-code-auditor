## Official Announcements

**[Code Review for Claude Code](https://claude.com/blog/code-review)** — Anthropic launched Code Review, a multi-agent system that dispatches teams of AI agents on every PR to catch bugs human reviewers miss. Agents work in parallel, cross-verify findings to minimize false positives, and deliver results as a summary comment plus inline PR annotations. Performance numbers from the launch: 84% of large PRs (1,000+ lines) get findings averaging 7.5 issues; less than 1% of findings are marked incorrect by engineers. Reviews cost $15–25 per PR. Available now in research preview for Team and Enterprise plans.
