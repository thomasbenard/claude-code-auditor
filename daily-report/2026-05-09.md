## Security

**[Anthropic's Claude used in attempted compromise of Mexican water utility](https://www.cybersecuritydive.com/news/anthropics-claude-compromise-mexican-water-utility/819710/)** (Cybersecurity Dive) — Dragos researchers documented a months-long campaign (Dec 2025 – Feb 2026) by an unidentified group targeting nine Mexican government agencies. Attackers used Claude Code and GPT-4.1 to conduct OT reconnaissance on a water utility — identifying an industrial gateway and attempting a password spray — despite lacking specialist ICS expertise. The OT breach failed. Significance: AI tooling lowered the skill floor for critical infrastructure attacks.
